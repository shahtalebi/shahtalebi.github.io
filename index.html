About Me

As a Senior Machine Learning Scientist at Unity Technologies, I specialize in Machine Learning, Generative AI, and Statistical Signal Processing. My professional journey has been marked by a deep commitment to advancing the field of AI through innovative solutions in Structure from Motion, 3D Reconstruction, and Computer Vision.

With proficient skills in Python programming, I have developed and deployed advanced models on platforms such as Google Cloud Platform and Microsoft Azure, utilizing best practices in MLOps, Kubernetes, and continuous integration and delivery (CI/CD) processes. This website showcases my journey, contributions, and the continuous pursuit of knowledge in the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
